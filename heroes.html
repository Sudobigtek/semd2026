South East Heroes of Migration Award 2026

Celebrating excellence in migration governance, youth empowerment, and community transformation

A Prestigious Recognition of Excellence

The South East Heroes of Migration Award is a prestigious and credible recognition that celebrates individuals and institutions whose work significantly advances safe, regular, and productive migration while addressing its challenges in the South East region of Nigeria. This award honours those who have transformed the migration narrative from one of loss and peril to one of opportunity, development, and dignity.

Whether through diaspora engagement, remittance mobilization, skills transfer, brain gain initiatives, creation of safe migration pathways, successful reintegration of returnees, or innovative approaches to reducing irregular migration and brain drain, our Heroes have demonstrated that migration can be a powerful force for good when governed wisely and supported compassionately.

The award seeks to identify, celebrate, and amplify the voices of role models who inspire positive migration narratives and accelerate South East development through their exceptional contributions.

Selection Criteria

What we look for in our Heroes

Impact on Migration

Direct or indirect contribution to diaspora engagement, remittances, skills transfer, brain gain, safe migration pathways, reintegration, or reducing irregular migration and brain drain.

Youth Development Focus

Demonstrated commitment to youth empowerment through education, scholarships, skills training, employment creation, or mentorship programs.

South East Connection

Strong ties to the South East as an indigene, through operations, philanthropy, or major impact in Abia, Anambra, Ebonyi, Enugu, or Imo State.

Leadership & Integrity

Demonstrated leadership with measurable contributions in philanthropy, business, advocacy, sports, arts, or policy influence, underpinned by integrity.

Diversity & Inclusion

We value balance across gender, sectors (business, faith, sports, arts, diaspora, public service), and generations.

Inspirational Value

Role models who promote positive migration narratives, inspire others to contribute to South East development, and embody the spirit of service.
