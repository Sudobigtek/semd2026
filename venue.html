Event Venue

International Conference Center Umuahia, Abia State, Nigeria

The International Conference Center Umuahia provides a world-class facility for hosting high-level dialogues and conferences. Located in the heart of Abia State capital, the venue offers state-of-the-art amenities for delegates.

  • Modern conference halls with capacity for 500+ delegates
  • High-speed internet and audio-visual equipment
  • Ample parking space and accessibility features
  • On-site catering and refreshment facilities
  • Proximity to hotels and transportation hubs
  • 24/7 security and emergency services

Venue Information

Getting There

The venue is easily accessible by road from all major cities in the South East. It's located approximately 1 hour from Sam Mbakwe Airport, Owerri.

Accommodation

Several hotels are available within 5-10 minutes of the venue. Special rates have been negotiated for SEMD 2026 delegates.

Dining

Lunch and refreshments will be provided during the event. The venue also has restaurants and cafes nearby.

Directions
  • From Aba: Take Aba-Umuahia Expressway (approx. 45 mins)
  • From Owerri: Take Owerri-Umuahia Road (approx. 1 hour)
  • From Enugu: Take Enugu-Port Harcourt Expressway (approx. 2 hours)
  • From Onitsha: Via Awka-Umuahia Road (approx. 1.5 hours)
